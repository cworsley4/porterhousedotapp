Enabling Automation Permissions

Tired of seeing this error?


It occurs when Porterhouse has been denied the ability to control the Messages app. But you can fix it :) Here is how...


  1. Open System Preferences
  2. Open the Security & Privacy pane
  3. At the top select "Privacy" > Scroll down to "Automation" and find Porterhouse. Make sure "System Events" and "Messages" are checked.

That should do it, go back and send your message.
